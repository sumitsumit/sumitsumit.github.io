I Sumit: Original Artwork by Sumit Basu

Sumit is a Seattle-based artist working primarily in three styles: digital oil, illustration, and vector. His subject matter spans a wide range, from whimsy to social justice to personal narrative. If you are interested in seeing more of his illustrative/narrative work, you may be interested in A Bear's Journey.
If you are interested in purchasing or licensing any of this work, please contact Sumit at sumit@thatsgreat.org.
