The bear crawled into his comfy bed as the rain started in earnest again; he listened to the sound of the raindrops against his window and thought about all he had been through that day. “So much happened today,” he thought to himself, “I really feel like I’ve embarked on a journey. Maybe I’ll find out what that gap in my heart line means after all. There’s so much to process, though, I need to talk about this all with some old friends who know me to understand what it all means.” And with that, he drifted off to sleep. #abearsjourney #31
